Stories

The Last Letter of Basantapur

A forgotten postbox, a promise, and a square that remembers.

The Girl Who Collected Sunsets

She stored golden skies in glass jars.

The Clockmaker of Patan

He fixed time but never chased it.

Lanterns Over the River

A silent wish carried by water.

The Tea Stall Philosopher

Where conversations simmered slowly.

The Mountain That Remembered

Echoes carried by ancient peaks.

A Library Without Doors

Where stories belonged to everyone.

The Boy Who Drew Silence

Art louder than words.

The Forgotten Courtyard

Silence breaks where laughter once lived.

Rain on Temple Steps

A storm becomes a quiet prayer.

The Weaver’s Promise

Threads stronger than distance.

Letters Never Sent

Words that waited too long.

The Silent Boatman

He carried people and quiet alike.

Echoes in the Alley

Walls that remember voices.

The Orchard Keeper

Care without claim.

The Sky Between Us

Distance measured in stars.

Midnight in Thamel

When lights dimmed, words glowed.

The Sparrow’s Return

Home reshaped but remembered.

A Window Facing East

Hope enters with morning light.

The Bridge of Paper Boats

Fragile dreams still float.

The Woman Who Wrote in Margins

Stories between the lines.

The Candle in the Storm

Small light, steady courage.

A Map Without Borders

Land without division.

The Last Storyteller

Voices carried forward.

When the Bells Fell Silent

Resilience in resonance.

The Rooftop Garden

Green above grey.

The Man Who Painted Rain

Storms in softer shades.

The House of Warm Bread

Comfort in crust and crumb.

Where the Road Bent Twice

Detours that define us.

The Shadow of the Mango Tree

Stories carried by branches and breeze.
